Who am I?

Hello, my name is Sergio Pacheco Ortiz. I am a sophomore at Mount St. Joseph University and I study music and Computer science.
I am from Cincinnati,OH and live at home with my family including, both parents, two younger sisters, and four cats.
Some of my hobbies include playing music on various different instruments. I play the saxophone, clarinet, electric bass, and trumpet.
Saxophone and clarinet are my most comfortable instruments. I have played clarinet since high school, and played tenor sax since junior year of high school.
I started learning electric bass around the same time as the sax but didn't really improve until i started college. I don't play much trumpet but i have performed it during college.
I am also on the Wrestling team at Mount St. Joseph University. I have been wrestling for four years, having started my sophomore year in high school.
I am also involved in different band ensembles at MSJ. I am in Concert band, Jazz and pep band, and steel drum band.
